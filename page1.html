BARE BACKSTREETS

A priceless amulet shatters after a scramble between treasure hunters, seemingly imbued by magic, wisps of essence dance into the sky, dispersing over the city skyline. The spirit of Venus, the god of fertility has reawakened, imbuing you and your friends with irresistible charm.

Vergence, a feisty green raptor who has recently been on a bad streak with women, deems the events a blessing. He is quick to realize however, that the effects of the charming spell has no restraints. Every creature in proximity has placed a target of ultimate desire on Verge and his allies, by any means necessary.
